BIO

My name is Jehan Kobe Chang.

I am currently a freshman undergraduate at UC Irvine majoring in Computer Science. I am also in the Campuswide Honors Program (CHP) -- which requires me to write blogs. Here is my resume and if you would like to know more about me, my email is jkobechang@gmail.com or Facebook message me @ Kobe Chang.

When I was a junior attending Henry M. Gunn High School, I founded and presided over the Gunn Virtual Reality Club. A year later, I published my VR game onto Steam. Now, I am a Programming Officer for VR @ UCI.

I have had two paid summer internships at Amplify.ai This company specializes in "AI-driven conversations" and makes Facebook chatbots for companies and campaigns such as Cheetos, Sephora, 48 Months, Flawless, etc.

Artificial Intelligence and its infinite applications have captured my imagination and inspired me to pursue this field. I aim to develop projects that utilize AI's capabilities in generating positive social, technological, and environmental impacts on the world (with a little VR thrown into the mix).
