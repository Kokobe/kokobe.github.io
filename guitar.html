Guitar VR
The goal of "Guitar VR" is to enable its users to play the chords from any song without any experience. It has potential applications in physical therapy, music creation, and inclusivity. For example, amputees can customize the controls of the app to play the guitar in a way that fits their bodies. Of course, this is just my vision and my app still has a long way to go. I'm very proud of the progress I was able to make during my free time in the past two weeks.
If you want to use this project or collaborate on it, here's the github repository! If you want to try it out directly: I posted a build on this repository. Email me at jkobechang@gmail.com if you have any questions or want more information!
